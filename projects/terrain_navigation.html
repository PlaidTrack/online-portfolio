Dynamic Terrain Navigation

An application of A* pathfinding

December 2022
Video & Report

As a final project for my computer animation course, I wanted to combine the ideas and concepts I've been learning throughout the semester and find an application with them. After some consulting with my professor, I decided to utilize B-splines and spherical linear interpolation and apply it on a path generated using A* pathfinding, an algorithm commonly used in video game AI.
