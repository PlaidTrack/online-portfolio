Weaver

A 2D sidescroller

February 2023
itch.io page

For the 2023 Chillennium Game Jam, me and a team of buddies developed a sidescrolling platformer using Unity 2D. The theme for this jam was "We share a heart," and after some discussion, we decided to make a reverse horror game where you play as a heart-shaped parasitic creature that wouldn't be out of place in a John Carpenter film. The main mechanic for this game is to that take control of enemies and adobt their abilities, but because we only had 48 hours we were only able to create one enemy type to host.
