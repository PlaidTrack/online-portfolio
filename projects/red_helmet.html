Red Helmet

A headpiece for cosplay

October 2023

Throughout September and October I played the game Bomb Rush Cyberfunk, and wanting to cosplay as a character named Red, I went to work at my local makerspace. I refreshed my knowledge on CAD, learned how to sauter wires to fix up a voice modulator, welding sheets of metal together, and cutting vinyl designs for t-shirts.
