Nomination Exchange Website

A simple top-down hack and slash

July 2023

The Center for International Business Studies (CIBS) requested an online interface to collect and store student requirement/preference data from exchange partners in order to optimize nominations for them. This would include some kind of form available to the exchange partners to fill in and submit successfully to CIBS. In turn, the data would be collected in a central and easily usable format, such as a spreadsheet, for the CIBS to view.


My main role for this project was to design and implement the frontend of the website, along with processing, storing and presenting the student/preference data in a usable format.
