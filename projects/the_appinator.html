The Appinator

A simple top-down hack and slash

July 2023
itch.io page

One of my goals in game development is to familarize myself with a wide range of game engines, so after a handful of tutorials I created this top-down hack and slash game using GameMaker. This is mostly a proof of concept largely inspired by the roguelike game Enter the Gungeon, specifically the movement and melee attack patterns.
